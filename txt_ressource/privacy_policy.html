2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve website functionality.

3. Sharing Your Information

4. Data Security

We use encryption and secure protocols to protect your data. However, no system is entirely secure, and we encourage you to use strong passwords.

5. Your Rights

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
